A letter to start off our 2nd Monthsary

Kay bilis ng panahon bebi!! another month down. As always, I'm more than thankful to have you in my life. I appreciate lahat ng mga efforts na ginagawa mo para sakin pero I salute you sa part na you always remain calm no matter how hard the situation is. For this letter, I would like to highlight how amazed I am kung paano mo ako ihandle. Kung ano anong nasasabi ko pero you were there saying "I Love You." Kahit anong galit ko during those moments, just to let you know na lumalambot puso ko tuwing naririnig ko sayo yun. Kahit gaano pa sumpungin anger issues ko, there's always this side of soft side of me. Just to let you know na the moment na lumamig na yung isip ko, sobra akong nagsisisi sa mga nagawa ko habang galit ako. Ganun pa man, let's continue na iachieve lahat ng mga pangarap natin sa buhay. Patuloy nating paglingkuran si God. Maraming salamat bebi! I'm always trying to get that best version of myself bebi. I LOVE YOU!

Laban lang! yayaman din tayo HAHAHAHHA.
Mahal na mahal kita ngayon at kailanman.
