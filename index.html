About Us

gabgab & kyky

We met at the Santuario de la Inmaculada Conception Parish, where I was captivated by your beauty. When I first saw you, it was like I found a priceless gem. While I was singing in the choir loft, I was captivated as you served at the altar. Suddenly I felt something strange, a feeling that my dream had come true. When I saw you, it was like I saw my future in you.

Our first official date - We went to SM Telabastagan and had a picture taken at a studio and then we went to Holy Angel University to celebrate University Days. We walked around and ate musubi. We also watched a concert by one of my favorite artists, Johnoy Danao "Ikaw at ako." And after everything, we went to Lala Garden and had coffee with my family.

Baguio Trip - One of our happiest vacations was going to Baguio city, we went to different attractions there and laughed. As I see your smiles, I see my life in you. Bonus factor is also that my family is there with us and they showed how supportive they are for our relationship.

Dungan - One of our favorite spots where we chill, talk about life, and visualize our future. The place where I taught you how to drive "roxrox." A very important place that I will keep in my heart.

Talimundoc San Miguel - Corazon De Jesus - The place where we entered a new chapter (courting stage). This is the place where I played a song for you and played a video to ask your permission if I can court you. Talimundoc SM also serves as a place where we stroll.

San Vicente Calius - Probably my favorite spot! This is the place where we usually eat chicken fillet that we bought from plaza. "Tambays" also happen here where we just chill, talk, and admire the beauty of nature.

SM Pampanga and Sky Ranch - I had so much fun here! Especially we are with my family. We went to different stores and ate. After everything, we went to Sky Ranch and had a chance to ride FIELDRUNNER and PAMPANGA'S EYE. SUPER FUN!!

Meeting You Always - It became a routine for us to meet everyday, despite our heavy and tiring day, we will still make time to meet always. Regardless of my evening classes, I will still reserve energy to be with you even for a short period of time. YOU WILL ALWAYS BE MY "PAHINGA."

Pares - Our very own PARES!!! Best nights, for real. We would eat pares, chill, and talk. I love these moments, simple but makes me very happy.

Always proud of you - Just to let you know that I'm always proud of you baby, I've witnessed your improvements and everything. I saw your sleepless nights and sacrifices for your studies and your gabgab.
