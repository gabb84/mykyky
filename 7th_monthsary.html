Starting things off, I just want to say that I'm the luckiest human being here on earth. The way you care, guide, and love me shows how real unconditional love works. Hindi natin kailangan maging mayaman, kahit saktuhan lang, basta masaya tayo, that's what matters most. Always remember na I will always be here, someone that will support, protect, guide, and love you. I will always be your partner. Happy 7th Monthsary bebi! I Love Youuuuu!
